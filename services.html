Organization Verification Services

EKEZA HOME is dedicated to helping you verify the legal status and registration of organizations, protecting you from fraud. Below are the methods we use to verify organization legitimacy on your behalf:

Official Government Registries

EKEZA HOME will conduct searches of official government registries, such as the Registrar of Companies in Kenya, to verify an organization's legitimacy. We will conduct searches through their online portals and/or visit their offices.

  • Online Search: EKEZA HOME will visit the official government website for business registration.
  • Physical Visit: EKEZA HOME will go to the offices of the Registrar of Companies.
  • Contacting Directly: EKEZA HOME will reach out to the registry via phone or email.

Licensing and Regulatory Bodies

EKEZA HOME, in partnership with the government, will access data from various licensing and regulatory bodies to verify if an organization is properly licensed and regulated within its industry.

  • Industry Regulator Websites: EKEZA HOME will check the official websites of the relevant regulatory bodies.
  • Direct Contact: EKEZA HOME will contact the regulatory body to confirm licensing.

Due Diligence Services

EKEZA HOME offers comprehensive due diligence services. Our experienced team conducts in-depth checks into an organization's background, legal status, and financial health, providing you with detailed and reliable information.

  • Company Background Checks: Verification of company registration, history, and ownership.
  • Legal Status Checks: Examination of legal documents, court records, and compliance.
  • Financial Health Analysis: Review of financial statements, assets, and liabilities.
  • Risk Assessment: Identification of potential risks and liabilities associated with the organization.

Public Records and Databases

EKEZA HOME will conduct searches of various public records and databases to provide information about an organization's legal status, including court records, legal notices, and other official publications.

  • Gazette Notices: EKEZA HOME will check the Kenya Gazette for any legal notices.
  • Court Records: EKEZA HOME will search court databases for any legal proceedings.
